üìã How Staking Works

Step 1: Connect Your Wallet
Connect your MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet containing IDIOT tokens to the staking platform.

Step 2: Choose Your Amount
Select how many IDIOT tokens you want to stake. You can stake any amount - no minimum required!

Step 3: Pick Your Lock Period
Choose from 30, 90, 180, or 365 days. Longer periods = higher APY rewards!

Step 4: Confirm Transaction
Approve the transaction in your wallet. Your tokens will be locked and start earning immediately.

Step 5: Earn Daily Rewards
Rewards accrue every day and can be claimed anytime. Unclaimed rewards auto-compound!

Step 6: Unstake When Ready
After your lock period ends, withdraw your principal + rewards. Or restake for continued earnings!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is staking? ‚ñº
Staking is the process of locking your IDIOT tokens in a smart contract for a set period to earn rewards. It's similar to earning interest in a savings account, but with cryptocurrency. Your tokens remain yours and are returned when the lock period ends.
Is there a minimum amount to stake? ‚ñº
No! You can stake any amount of IDIOT tokens. Whether you have 100 or 1,000,000 tokens, everyone can participate and earn rewards proportional to their stake.
Can I unstake early? ‚ñº
Yes, but there's a penalty. Early unstaking incurs a 5-20% penalty depending on your chosen lock period. The penalty goes back into the reward pool for other stakers. It's best to choose a lock period you're comfortable with.
How are rewards calculated? ‚ñº
Rewards are calculated daily based on your staked amount and chosen APY tier. For example, if you stake 10,000 IDIOT at 50% APY (365 days), you earn approximately 13.7 IDIOT per day. Rewards accrue automatically and can be claimed anytime.
Are the smart contracts audited? ‚ñº
Yes! Before mainnet launch, all staking contracts will undergo professional security audits by reputable firms like CertiK or Quantstamp. Audit reports will be publicly available. Security is our top priority.
What happens to my rewards if I don't claim them? ‚ñº
Unclaimed rewards automatically compound! They continue earning rewards on top of your original stake. This maximizes your returns over time. You can claim whenever you want without affecting your staked principal.
Can I add more tokens to my existing stake? ‚ñº
Yes! You can create multiple stakes or add to existing ones. Each stake has its own lock period and rewards. This gives you flexibility to manage your portfolio and take advantage of different APY tiers.
Where do the rewards come from? ‚ñº
Rewards come from a dedicated 50M IDIOT treasury allocation (5% of total supply). This ensures sustainable rewards for years. The treasury is managed by a multisig wallet and distributed algorithmically by smart contracts.
