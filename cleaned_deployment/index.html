ü§Ø Idiot's Crash Course to Crypto

New here? Don't sweat it. We've put together a simple guide to help you understand the basics of crypto before you dive in. Start here, and you'll sound like a pro in no time.

A crypto wallet is like your bank account, but for digital assets. It lets you send, receive, and store tokens. Examples: MetaMask (software) or Ledger (hardware).
Ethereum (ETH) is one of the biggest cryptocurrencies. On Uniswap, ETH is usually wrapped into WETH (Wrapped ETH) to make it compatible with smart contracts.
Tokens are digital assets created on blockchains. They can represent projects, communities, or even memes (like IDIOT Token üöÄ). They trade just like ETH, but usually live on top of a blockchain.
An LP is where users deposit tokens into a shared pool on Uniswap. Traders swap against that pool, and LPs earn a cut of the fees (0.3% per trade on IDIOT Token).
Gas fees are the network costs paid to process your transaction. On Base (an Ethereum L2), gas fees are cheap compared to main Ethereum.
IDIOT Token doesn't skim a cut from your transfers. You keep 100% of what you buy, sell, or send. The only fees are Uniswap's 0.3% trading fee, which goes to liquidity providers, not to the dev team.

üîó What is Base Network?

Base is a Layer 2 blockchain built by Coinbase. It's faster and cheaper than Ethereum mainnet, but still secure. Think of it as a "fast lane" for Ethereum transactions.

üí∞ What is WETH?

WETH = Wrapped Ethereum. When you send ETH to Uniswap, it automatically converts to WETH for trading. It's the same value as ETH, just in a format that works with smart contracts.

üîÑ What is a Token Pair?

IDIOT/ETH means you can trade between IDIOT tokens and Ethereum. You can buy IDIOT with ETH, or sell IDIOT back to ETH anytime at equal value.

üíé Why Equal Value?

The liquidity pool ensures fair pricing. If IDIOT price goes up, you get more ETH when selling. If it goes down, you get less ETH. It's always 1:1 value exchange.
