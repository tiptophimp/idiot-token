IDIOT Airdrop Rules

These rules govern the Idiocracy airdrop distribution. Read carefully.

Eligibility

  • Wallets must be present in the official allowlist/merkle set.
  • No sanctions or prohibited jurisdictions. One claim per wallet.

Claim Window

  • Claims are available during the published window. Late claims may be rejected.
  • Gas fees are paid by the claimer (ETH on Base).

Proofs & Transparency

  • Official proofs file: /airdrop/idiocracy-1.json
  • Amounts and addresses are fixed for this drop; any change requires republishing the proofs.

Security

  • Always verify the token contract address: 0xC29EF04CFFe38012dcfc1E96a2B368443f298dE1
  • Beware of DM scams. We will never ask for your seed phrase or keys.
